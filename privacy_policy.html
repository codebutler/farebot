Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 22, 2026

FareBot is an open-source transit card reader. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data FareBot handles and how.

Data We Do Not Collect

FareBot does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Specifically:

  • No analytics or tracking. We do not use any analytics services.
  • No crash reporting. Crash data stays on your device.
  • No accounts or sign-ups. The app does not require or support user accounts.
  • No advertising. There are no ads in the app.
  • No server communication. FareBot does not send data to any server we operate.

Data Stored on Your Device

When you scan a transit card, the following data is stored locally on your device:

  • Raw card data (the binary content read from the card)
  • Card serial number
  • Card type
  • Scan timestamp
  • Encryption keys (if provided for locked cards)

This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it using the share or export feature.

Third-Party Services

Google Maps (Android only)

The Android version uses Google Maps to display transit station locations. When you view a map, your device sends location coordinates to Google to load map tiles. This is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

No other third-party services are used.

Hardware Access

  • NFC: Used to read transit cards. Card data is processed and stored locally.
  • Bluetooth: Used on iOS to connect to Flipper Zero devices for importing NFC dumps. No data is transmitted over the internet.
  • USB: The web version uses WebUSB to connect to USB NFC readers. All processing happens locally in your browser.

Data Export

You can export your saved card data to files. Exported data is handled by your device's native sharing functionality. FareBot does not control where you send exported files.

Open Source

FareBot is open source. You can review the complete source code at github.com/codebutler/farebot to verify these claims.

Changes

If this policy changes, we will update the "Last updated" date above.
